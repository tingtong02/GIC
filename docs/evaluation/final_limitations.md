# Final Limitations

## Data Limits

- Final synthetic results still depend on a small controlled benchmark.
- Final real-event evidence is based on a small frozen event set.

## Physics Limits

- The physics path still uses simplified assumptions and proxy field reconstruction for real events.
- Real-event inference is not a direct full-grid truth evaluation.

## KG Limits

- KG is retained as an optional enhancement, not a universally promoted default.
- The final system does not claim that KG always improves every evaluation surface.

## Real Validation Limits

- Evidence is currently bounded to station-local disturbance references.
- Current real-event findings must not be interpreted as production-grade deployment readiness.
